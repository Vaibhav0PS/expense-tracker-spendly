Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when creating an account and logging expenses, including your name, email address, and any expense data you enter such as amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide and improve the Spendly service, including generating spending reports, category breakdowns, and summaries. We do not use your personal information for advertising or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your account data and expense records are stored securely. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@spendly.app. Upon deletion, your data will be removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third party services for hosting, analytics, and email delivery. These services are required to operate the application and may process your data as necessary to provide their services. We take reasonable steps to ensure any third parties we work with handle your data securely.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses cookies to maintain your login session and remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the service may not function properly without them.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@spendly.app.
